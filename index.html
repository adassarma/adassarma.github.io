About

Welcome to my little corner of the web! I am a graduate student at the Computer Science Department, School of Computer, Data & Information Sciences, University of Wisconsin - Madison.

I have been working on implementing a wifi packet sniffer using ESP32 to track the presence of unauthorized devices under the guidance of Prof Rahul Chatterjee. With a keen interest in high performance computing and Operating Systems, I am well versed in the C++ programming language. Additionally, I have three publications from my undergraduate research projects on topics such as Quantum Computing and Communication Systems. At TCS Research Bangalore, India, I contributed to the development of a novel approach for decoding LDPC codes using annealers. I spent the summer of '22 in Germany, working on a research project in computational chemistry to calculate total energy densities of Beryllium atoms using quantum kernels.

I am quite passionate about music and I love to interact with people.
